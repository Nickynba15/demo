ABOUT US

This is a form that we have generated to give out to people in relation to an organisation that we are starting. The organisation will be called ‘NORMALI’ and will listen to people and also provide legal as well as therapeutic aid to those who are under various psychological turmoils. It will function initially in the form of a testimony collection unit. You can share with us almost anything and your identity shall remain anonymous as long as you don’t mention your name or the place that you are from, in the testimony that you submit. You would attain legal or therapeutic aid only if you insist us to help you with it, by being ready to come out with your identity. Else, the testimony will be completely anonymous.
